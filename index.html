Fredj Moussa

Born 1992. Paris, France
Lives and works Sousse, Tunisia

He graduated from the École nationale supérieure des Arts Décoratifs in Paris in 2018, and resided at the Cité internationale des arts in Paris in 2019. He studied at Le Fresnoy from 2021 to 2023.

In 2024, he is a resident at Villa Medici in Rome.

Fredj Moussa’s practice unfolds at the intersection of film and sculpture. He creates landscapes where fiction becomes a mode of resistance.

Exhibitions include Berlin Biennale, Dakar, Tunis, Paris, Bern, Athens.

Films shown at international festivals including TIFF.

Awards include CIFRA prize (2023) and Best Experimental Documentary at Jihlava IDFF (2025).
